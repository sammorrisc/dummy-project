ABOUT SME COUNCIL "A NEW VOICE FOR SMALL BUSINESS ENTREPRENEURS"

BUSINESS CONSULTANT

USISME Business consultants work in areas that include marketing, human resources, management, and accounting. Consultants help improve companies by assessing weaknesses and recommending solutions.

INDUSTRY CONSULTANT

USISME Consultants offer advice and expertise to organisations to help them improve their business performance in terms of operations, profitability, management, structure and strategy.

BUSINESS PLANNING

USISME Business development plans provide guidance to organizations in purpose, including mission, vision and values, as well as product or service, target audience and the strategies they will use to achieve success.

ONLINE CONSULTANT

USISME Bridges everything your consultancy needs, in one place. Manage clients, projects, tasks, time, expenses and invoicing.

TAX PLANNING

USISME Tax planning involves conceiving of and implementing various strategies in order to minimize the amount of taxes paid for a given period. For a small business, minimizing the tax liability can provide more money for expenses & investment.

BANK INVESTMENT

USISME Has investment banking in special segment of banking operation that helps individuals or organisations raise capital and provide financial consultancy services to them.
